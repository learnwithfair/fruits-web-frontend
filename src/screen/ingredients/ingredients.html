Allergens & Special Diets

If you have a food allergy or any special dietary needs, please alert a Juice Generation manager. Our staff is routinely informed of the ingredients on our menu and will do their best to accommodate you. We do not assume any responsibility for a person’s sensitivity or allergy to any food item provided in our stores.

Kitchen operations may involve shared equipment, preparation areas, and utensils. We take great care to thoroughly clean equipment and surfaces however, despite our best efforts, the possibility remains that your food will come in contact with other food products or possible allergens.

Apple

Nutrient-dense apples are the most popular fruit around the world—and there’s a reason they say “an apple a day keeps the doctor away.” Green Granny Smith apples, specifically, offer 20% of your daily value of vitamin C, plus vitamin A, calcium, and iron. They’re also high in fiber, which can aid with weight loss, improved gut health, and lower cholesterol levels.

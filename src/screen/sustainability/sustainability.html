Local & Organic

As an independently owned company, we love supporting our local farmers and growers. We proudly invest in locally grown produce from sources we trust, allowing you to reap the benefits of fresher ingredients (and us to cut down on fuel costs). Rest assured, we know exactly where our leafy greens, cucumbers, berries, and beets come from so you can always feel confident in what you’re drinking.

Additionally, we commit to using organic produce whenever possible. Our cold-pressed juices and our production facility are all certified USDA Organic.
